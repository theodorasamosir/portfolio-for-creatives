  • About Me

    Hi, please call me Theo. I’d describe myself as shy around strangers online, and yet, ferociously passionate about my work. I love experimenting and sharing my process on my blog, and I love looking at how other people create and work.

    I like conquering difficult things that promise great rewards – the entire reason I focus on 3D. Making good work is never about instant gratification, but about what I learn from myself and others along the way.

    In my spare time I am probably drawing scratchy cartoons or having very, very strange dreams in my sleep.

    If you have any inquiries- any at all, not just about work availability -feel free to write me an email! I like hearing what other people think about the act of making art, no matter the medium.
